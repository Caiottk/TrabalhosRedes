Courage the Cowardly Dog

"Courage the Cowardly Dog" is an animated television series that originally aired from 1999 to 2002. The show follows the adventures of Courage, a small and timid pink dog who lives with an elderly couple, Muriel and Eustace Bagge, in a remote farmhouse in the middle of Nowhere, Kansas. Despite his fears, Courage often finds himself facing various supernatural and terrifying creatures to protect his owners and their home.
